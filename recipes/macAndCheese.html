Homemade Mac Miller and Cheese

Description

Homemade Mac Miller and Cheese is made from the remaining remnants of one of the most well known underground artists of our time. Well known in American households, and served well with mustard.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Water
  • Mac Miller

Steps

  1. Poor Water into a Pot
  2. Boil Water
  3. Pour Macaroni into Water
  4. Pour Mac Miller into Water
  5. Wait 2-3 minutes
  6. Drain the Pot and place remaining Pasta onto Plate
  7. Sprinkle Cheese onto the Pasta
