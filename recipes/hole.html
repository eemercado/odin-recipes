Homemade Hole in a Wall

Description

Very good addition to add to any type of house, no matter the cost or location. Warning, may lead to expensive damage inflicted onto a house if done correctly.

Ingredients

  • House
  • Wall
  • Hammer

Steps

  1. Grip Hammer with dominant hand
  2. Go to a wall and stay within optimal swinging distance
  3. Swing your hammer at the wall with full force
